Guidance

Suggested Path For New Players

Warm-Up

Learn the shape of broken queries

Focus on login, registration, and search. You should be able to see how quotes, comments, and UNION logic affect the response.

Intermediate

Move into authenticated surfaces

Use your account to explore notes, profile updates, sorting, pagination, and book lookups where payloads hit less obvious SQL fragments.

Chain

Store, trigger, and pivot

Practice second-order injection by planting input in one endpoint and detonating it through reporting, review search, or admin-only flows.
